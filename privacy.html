5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Globister uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (shopping cart, login).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences like language and region.
  • Targeting Cookies: Limited use for retargeting campaigns (you can opt out via your browser settings).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect site features. For more details, see our Cookie Preference Center (available in footer).
