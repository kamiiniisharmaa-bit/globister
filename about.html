Our cosmic origin

Lunar Emporium was founded by selenophile and designer Elara Voss after she spent a transformative year traveling under moonlit skies. Frustrated by mass-produced decor that lacked soul, she began hand-selecting ethically crafted artifacts — from meteorite-inspired jewelry to lunar-phase clocks — each piece telling a story of the night sky.

What started as a small Etsy shop has grown into a global community of dreamers, stargazers, and conscious consumers. Today, we partner with artisans across the globe who share our commitment to sustainable materials, fair wages, and celestial artistry.

Every product at Lunar Emporium is a small tribute to the moon’s quiet power — a reminder to pause, look up, and embrace the mystery.

Our guiding light

Ethical Craftsmanship

We partner with small-batch artisans and ensure fair wages, sustainable materials, and minimal waste. Every piece is made with respect for people and planet.

Curated Wonder

From moon-phase planners to selenite towers, our collection is thoughtfully chosen to spark joy, curiosity, and cosmic connection.

Earth-Conscious Orbit
